Emily Bradley
Owner and Certified Level 4 Pilates Instructor

Emily Bradley

Emily graduated cum laude from the university of Cincinnati conservatory of music in 1990, and has danced professionally for twelve years.

Emily received her pilates training in New York City, under Romana Kryzanowska, Master Teacher, the world-renowned protégé of Joseph Pilates.

Emily received her certification in 1999, and is a Certified Level 4 Pilates Instructor.

The Pilates Powerhouse is the only studio in Alpharetta and Roswell that teaches Classical Pilates as taught by Romana Kryzanowska. Emily has been teaching Pilates since 1998 and was hand picked by Romana to become a Level 4 Instructor.

Jeanne Deitrich
Certified Level 5+ Pilates Instructor

Jeanne Deitrich

Jeanne received her ballet training at the Atlanta Ballet Centre for Dance Education, Burklyn Ballet Theatre, Cincinnati Ballet and America Ballet Theatre and graduated from the University of Cincinnati College-Conservatory of Music with a Master of Arts degree in Arts Administration. She has worked in New York City with Frank Salomon Associates as a concert and artist manager, Eliot Feld as a ballet company manager, and with the New York City Ballet as a finance associate.

She began her Pilates training in New York to complement her ballet studies and continued after moving home to Atlanta. While recuperating from a severe dance injury, Jeanne realized how Pilates could strengthen her entire body while allowing for the healing process. She decided to become a Pilates instructor and received her certification with Romana's Pilates in 2003.

Sandra Benson

Sandra Benson, a consulting firm Vice President and a Pilates instructor/teacher trainer, understands first-hand the challenges and rewards of finding the time and energy for fitness in a busy, professional life. Sandra was initially certified and began teaching in 2001. In 2003, Sandra started training with Emily Bradley.

She fell in love with the classical method and technique and became a Romana's Pilates certified instructor in 2005. She advanced her education by attending seminars and continuing education workshops nationwide and became a leveled instructor in 2008. A former gymnast, she finds the studio a natural environment, but continues to balance the corporate world with the physical. This is reflected in her teaching style, which is informed by her business training and her passion for the classical Pilates method. Sandra holds an MBA from Georgia State University.

Jenny Dale

In 1998 Jenny started practicing Pilates at the Atlanta Ballet. She was fortunate enough to meet Romana Kryzanowska and observe her teaching there. Jenny is dedicated to the method and loves that anyone can practice Pilates no matter what age or shape they are in. A full workout can be given even when injured because the classical Pilates method focuses on the individual and correct form.

Jenny has a Masters Degree in Bioinformatics from Georgia Tech. She was a researcher in gene expression and disease at the Georgia Tech/Emory Department of Biomedical Engineering. Hours in front of a computer took a toll on her health. Jenny decided to get out from behind the desk and become a certified Romana's Pilates instructor. She apprenticed under Emily Bradley and Robin Warden Blum. Jenny has studied with several Romana's Pilates top teacher-trainers and tested at Joseph Pilates' original NY studio with Sari Mejia Santos, Jerome Weinberg and Jamie Trout. in 2012, she looks forward to sharing and expanding her knowledge and will be a lifelong student of Pilates.
