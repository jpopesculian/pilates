Class Descriptions

Mat 1 Beginner: An introduction to the basic mat exercises focusing on centering and alignment.
Mat 2 Intermediate: A faster moving class introducing the intermediate mat exercises with a focus on breathing and alignment
Mat 3 Advanced: A faster moving class focusing on the Advanced mat exercise with more emphasis on rhythm and flow (Instructor's approval required to take this class.)
Tower A challenging class that adds resistance by using Pilate's apparatus. Limited to three people. Call to reserve a spot.

Private Sessions on Pilates Equipment

Enjoy a private lesson on the Reformer. The Pilates equipment can either make the Pilates exercises more rehabilitative or more challenging, whatever is applicable to your situation. Since every person's body is different, this one-on-one attention will allow you to gain feedback on your specific postural needs.

Pilates on the Reformer gives you all the benefits of the mat with the additional bonus of working with or against resistance. One of the main advantages of the reformer is that it can act as a support for people who have illnesses or injuries and it can also make traditional mat exercises more difficult by providing resistance.

Private sessions are by appointment only. Please call or email for session availability.
