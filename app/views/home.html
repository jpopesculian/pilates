Our mission is to keep the integrity of Romana's Pilates alive, through a positive attitude and environment. Romana's Pilates is based on the original teachings of Joseph Pilates.

After 12 years of dancing professionally, I found Pilates - which was another way of moving and expressing my body while keeping strength and flexibility. It was also a way for me to help others learn to move efficiently in their daily lives. Pilates is my passion and I love helping others feel better."

Emily Bradley,
Owner and
